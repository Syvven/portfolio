Noah Hendrickson

I was first exposed to programming in my senior year of highschool when I attempted to teach myself C++, and, since then, I have taken various Computer Science courses and done various projects, all reinforcing my love for coding.

I also enjoy playing video games such as Destiny 2, Final Fantasy XIV, and BeatSaber. When I get the chance, I also enjoy traveling, and especially love mountain ranges. There's nothing quite like being able to see everything for miles around!
